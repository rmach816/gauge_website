GAUGE Beta App Description

GAUGE is your personal virtual tailor powered by AI. Get personalized clothing recommendations, outfit suggestions, and shopping links based on your exact measurements, style preferences, and wardrobe.

Key Features

Virtual Tailor Chat
Ask your AI tailor anything about clothing, style, and fit. The tailor knows your measurements and provides personalized recommendations.

Smart Size Recommendations
Get accurate size suggestions for shirts, pants, jackets, and shoes based on your body measurements and height.

Wardrobe Management
Add items to your digital wardrobe by taking photos. Organize and search your clothing collection by category.

Outfit Builder
Build complete outfits for any occasion. Mix items from your wardrobe or get shopping suggestions for missing pieces.

Shopping Integration
Shop for recommended items across 10 major retailers including Amazon, Nordstrom, J Crew, Target, and more. Get direct links to products that match your style and size.

Style Preferences
Set your style preferences from Conservative to Street. The AI uses your preferences to make better recommendations.

What to Expect in Beta

This is an early version of GAUGE. Some features may have minor issues. Shopping links may occasionally require manual search. The AI is learning and improving with each update.

Your feedback helps us improve GAUGE. Please report any issues you encounter, especially with size recommendations, shopping links, or the AI chat functionality.

Thank you for testing GAUGE.
